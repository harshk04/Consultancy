IB WORLD SCHOOLS

IB World Schools

We support IB World Schools and candidate schools by strengthening learning-centred systems, leadership coherence, and curriculum alignment.

SUPPORT

Where We Can Help

  • Programme evaluation readiness and evidence alignment
  • Curriculum intent, alignment, and assessment coherence
  • Leadership structures that support learning
  • Professional learning aligned to programme standards and practices

APPROACH

Working in Context

We approach IB contexts with care: respecting school culture, honouring programme identity, and co-creating strategies that are implementable with your teams.

Discuss IB Support

FOCUS AREAS

Focus Areas

Programme Standards & Practices

Clarifying evidence, strengthening routines, and aligning implementation with standards in a way that supports learning rather than compliance.

Curriculum Coherence

Shared intent, aligned progressions, and practical assessment routines that support consistent experiences for students.

Leadership and Culture

Distributed leadership structures, meeting routines, and reflection cycles that protect time for learning.

Professional Learning

Inquiry-based learning cycles that connect professional learning to classroom practice and evidence.

BEGIN

How to Begin

  • Share your context and programme priorities
  • Identify one or two focus areas for the initial scope
  • Agree timelines, rhythms, and who should be involved
  • Begin with listening, mapping, and a practical next-step plan
